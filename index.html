About — Vamshi Krishna

A studio for
slow images.

VK Lenscraft is a small, deliberate practice rooted in India. We make photographs the way other people make films — patiently, with the lens close, and the room quiet. No filters, no posing. Only light, geometry, and the long minute before the shutter.
